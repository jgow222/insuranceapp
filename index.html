The Problem

Insurance is built to minimize your payout.

When you file a claim, adjusters work for the insurer — not for you. The average person has no idea what their policy actually covers, no way to evaluate whether the initial offer is fair, and no leverage to push back.

"They accepted less money than they were owed because fighting back felt impossible without a lawyer."

80-page policies

Most people never read their insurance policy. Insurers count on that.

Low-ball first offers

Initial settlement offers are routinely 20–40% below what you're entitled to.

No leverage without a lawyer

Hiring an attorney means giving up 33% of your settlement. ClaimMax costs 10-15% — only if you win more.

How It Works

Five steps. One goal: more money.

ClaimMax walks you through every step. Our AI does the heavy lifting.

1

Upload your policy

Photograph or upload your policy PDF. AI extracts coverage limits, exclusions, deductibles, and endorsements in plain English — within 60 seconds.
